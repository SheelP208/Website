This is my Portfolio

Salad Project

Has a salad bar where the customer can choose what ingrediants and how much they want of it. Once done ordering, the customer pays and a thank you sign pops up. I used a variety of if statements for user input and functions/turtles to draw the ingrediants. I also used for loops for the amount of ingrediants dispensed.

Space Maze Project

Made a maze that represents space. Includes the user, follower, and point system. The point of the game is to get as many of the rockets as you can without hitting any walls or getting caught by the follower(UFO). After succefully catching the rocket, exit out the maze to get score. If the user is caught by the follower or hits any walls, it is game over and a message appears on the screen. The score shows up at the end of the game.

Christmas Joy E-Card

An E-card that starts out with a closed envelope and opens when clicked on. Once opened, the card pops up and consists of a winter background. Turtles are used to draw each part of the snowman and at the end a message pops up.
